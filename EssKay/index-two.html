About EssKay

Get full range of premium Fabrication services for your business

1989 was the year Ess Kay starting manufacturing copper components for the air conditioning and refrigeration industry. We started with simple copper tube bends using small manual benders in a small workshop. Its been a long but memorable journey of 27 years. Today Ess Kay Group is the leader in copper and brass component manufacturing for the home appliance and automobile industry.....
